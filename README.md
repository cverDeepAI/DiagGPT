
**A Vision-Language AI System for Medical Imaging Assistance**

---

## Overview

**DiagGPT** is a vision-language model (VLM) fine-tuning framework designed for medical imaging understanding and clinical question answering. Built on top of state-of-the-art multimodal large language models, DiagGPT aims to bring AI-assisted diagnostic capabilities to real-world clinical workflows.

The system supports multi-turn visual conversations grounded in medical images, enabling contextual reasoning and structured clinical responses.

## Status

> 🔒 **Code and data will be made available upon authorization in a future release.**

This repository currently serves as a research preview. The full training pipeline, model weights, and curated medical imaging datasets are undergoing internal review and compliance evaluation before public release.

We appreciate your interest and patience.

## Features (Preview)

- Fine-tuning pipeline for vision-language models on medical imaging data
- Support for multi-image and single-image conversation formats
- Structured think-and-answer response format for clinical reasoning
- Data quality verification and automated correction pipeline
- Compatible with Qwen-VL series model architectures
